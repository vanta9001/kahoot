Frequently asked questions

What customization options are available with Kahoot! 360 Pro Max?

Kahoot! 360 Pro Max allows you to customize the look and feel of your Kahoot! experience. You can define your brand colors and create multiple custom backgrounds to use in your kahoots.

How many licenses do I need?

You need one license per presenter. A presenter is someone who creates and presents kahoots to colleagues or assigns self-paced content. Teams with more than one presenter must purchase a separate license for each presenter. Participants joining a kahoot (ie. joining the game via the PIN code) don’t require an account or license.

Do I have to pay for participants in a kahoot session?

No, participants don’t need an account or a business license to join kahoots.

How many participants can join a kahoot session?

This varies by plan. The maximum number of simultaneous participants per session is 2,000, which is possible with Kahoot! 360 Pro Max.

I can't find my current plan in the offer.

Do you have an active business subscription that isn’t visible on the pricing pages? Check the features of archived and old plans.  

Can I share a login with a colleague?

No, each Kahoot! license is limited to one user as per our Terms and Conditions. Each license holder is responsible for preventing access to their account by anyone else. Sharing an account will impact the user experience and may lead to restricted platform access. Instead, you should purchase multiple licenses.

Can I use Kahoot! in languages other than English?

Yes! You can write questions and answers in any language. You can also play, create, and host kahoots, view reports, and more in the following languages: Spanish, French, Brazilian Portuguese, German, Italian, Norwegian, Swedish, Dutch, Turkish, Polish, Japanese, Arabic, Ukrainian, Korean, Indonesian, Simplified Chinese, Traditional Chinese, Thai, and Vietnamese.

Can I sign up for free?

Yes! You can get a free account. You can also test drive Kahoot! 360 Pro Standard for free. No credit card needed. Our demo plan lets you try creating a kahoot with advanced features from Kahoot! 360 Pro Standard, our most popular plan for presentations.

Explore our library of advanced features, such as brainstorms, polls, and quiz questions, or have a go at creating a course to test out with 3 participants. You can also present selected ready-made kahoots from our business collection for up to 50 participants.

Once you’ve created your premium kahoots, you can upgrade to a paid Kahoot! 360 Pro plan at any time and host your kahoots for up to 2000 participants. Get started.

Can I use Kahoot! to engage my customers or prospects?

To use Kahoot! for marketing, sales, advertising, and customer-facing purposes, you’ll need a Kahoot! 360 Pro Max, Kahoot! for Brands, or Enterprise plan. This includes activities such as trade shows, conferences, product launches, recruitment, consultancy services, or sessions to enhance customer experience. All Kahoot! 360 plans can be used for training and communication among employees, freelancers, and partners.

Do you offer any discounts for nonprofits or NGOs?

Yes! Registered nonprofits and NGOs can get a 50% discount. For more information, visit our nonprofit page.

I need Kahoot! for just one event. Is there a separate pricing plan for that?

Yes! See our pricing for one-off events. 

How do all the features work?

Check out our YouTube playlist with a series of video tutorials that will show you how you get the most out of Kahoot!, including how to brand your kahoots, assign challenges, facilitate brainstorms, and more! You can also find more information on our resources page.

What is an interactive presentation?

You can import existing slides or create new ones in Kahoot! and then add interactive questions and polls to create engaging presentations that everyone remembers. We call these interactive presentations. Our slide importer supports .ppt (PowerPoint), PDF (you can export Google Slides as PDFs), and .key (Keynote) formats. Learn more about creating interactive presentations with Kahoot!.

Can I use Kahoot! to survey the audience?

Yes, you can gather participants’ feedback, opinions, and ideas in real-time with polls, word clouds, open-ended questions, and brainstorms.

What is the player identifier?

The player identifier allows trainers and admins to identify individual participants when they join a new kahoot session, so you know exactly who it is beyond the anonymous on-screen nickname. When joining a new session, participants are required to enter their email address, which is then used for learner tracking and reporting. When joining via our mobile app, the email address is saved from the first time they enter it, so they won’t have to enter it when joining your kahoots in the future.

What is a Kahoot! Tournament?

A tournament is a great way to build engagement across a series of kahoot sessions. In the Reports section, you can create a tournament leaderboard by combining scores from multiple kahoots into one report which you can share with participants. You can only combine reports from kahoots that had the “player identifier” feature on, as player identifiers are necessary to collate data.
